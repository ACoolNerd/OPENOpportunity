Retail protocol

Approval, inventory and settlement

1. Stephen approval

Every product intended for OPEN must be reviewed and approved before it reaches the store floor.

2. SKU + passport

Every pack and card receives a unique SKU, pack ID and QR product passport before placement.

3. Square + QuickBooks

Square records checkout; QuickBooks remains the accounting system. No duplicate payment-processing fee is applied.

4. Monthly allocation

OPEN receives $5 for the first 50 qualifying packs monthly, then $1 per qualifying pack until reset.

5. 90-day rotation

Unsold singles and packs are reviewed, repriced, rotated or returned at the 90-day checkpoint.

6. Super-admin controls

Stephen Smalls and Keith McPherson have super-admin roles. Passwords remain outside source code and MFA is required.
