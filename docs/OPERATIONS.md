# OPEN Opportunity operating protocol

## Authority and partners

- Keith McPherson / ACoolNERD: ACoolCOLLECTOR owner and Collectors Co-op co-founder; product, inventory, technology, QR, reporting, and brand super-admin.
- Stephen Smalls: OPEN owner and Collectors Co-op co-founder; OPEN retail, vendor program, merchandising, custody, community, and operations super-admin.
- Every product proposed for the OPEN floor requires Stephen's recorded approval before it is published to Square or displayed.

## Approved economics

- Mystery packs: $29.99 Standard, $49.99 Premium, $79.99 Chase.
- OPEN monthly pack allocation: `MIN(units, 50) × $5 + MAX(units − 50, 0) × $1`.
- Mystery packs: above allocation plus 5% of net pack sales.
- Raw singles: 25% of net card sales.
- Manufacturer-sealed: 20% of net sales.
- Net sales exclude sales tax, shipping, refunds, chargebacks, and the actual payment-processing fee. Do not deduct a second “QuickBooks fee” when Square processed the payment.

## 90-day OPEN floor cycle

1. ACool proposes pack/single/sealed SKU with photo, condition, owner, price, and source.
2. Stephen approves, rejects, or requests changes.
3. Assign ACool ID, Square Item ID, QuickBooks Item ID, custody status, and QR passport.
4. Place up to 40 visible raw cards with 10 reserve cards inside the 3 × 3-foot footprint.
5. Reconcile sales/refunds weekly and settlements monthly.
6. Rotate, reprice, or return unsold inventory at day 90.

## Pack production

Use opaque 4 × 6 or 4.5 × 7-inch heat-sealable metallized pouches. Use the FoodSaver heat bar only after destructive testing on empty pouches and low-value commons; never vacuum cards. Sleeve all cards, protect hits, record manifest, and apply a serialized tamper seal.

The proposed Juice Up/soursop campaign is disabled by default. It may not launch until the legal sponsor, prize source, dates, territory, eligibility, free entry route, privacy owner, fulfillment, and Official Rules are approved in writing. A purchase may not improve odds.
