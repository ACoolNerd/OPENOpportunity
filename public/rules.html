Community Campaign Official Rules

This campaign is intentionally disabled. These rules are a review checklist, not final legal terms.

Required before launch

  1. Identify legal sponsor and administrator.
  2. Define exact start/end time and eligible territory/ages.
  3. State “No Purchase Necessary” and provide a free entry route with equal odds.
  4. Describe prize, quantity, approximate retail value, restrictions, and fulfillment.
  5. State selection, notification, verification, publicity, privacy, tax, and dispute terms.
  6. Obtain OPEN approval and qualified promotions counsel review.

No purchase may improve odds. Pack purchases and the promotional entry must remain operationally separate.
